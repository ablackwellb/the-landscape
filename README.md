# The Landscape
### The path does not erase the canyons. It remains unbroken through them.
 
> Life is hard and beautiful at the same time. Sometimes we are in the midst of something heavy… so heavy that we are unable to step back and see our life from afar. **This tool exists for that step back.**
 
The Landscape turns your life's moments into a three-dimensional terrain. The hard seasons, full of pain or loss, become valleys — rendered at their true depth, never flattened. The times you have thrived become peaks that catch the light. A continuous path runs through all of it, because that is what a life is: one unbroken line through everything.
Every depth and every height is placed by your hand. The land only renders what you already know.
---

## What this Is and what it Isn’t

This is a personal reflection tool. You enter your own moments, you decide where each one lives, and the terrain shows you your own life from a vantage point daily life doesn't offer.
This is never therapy, assessment, or advice. It does not score you, interpret you, measure your resilience, or tell you what your moments mean. It makes no claims about your future. You are the only authority on your own life — the tool's entire design exists to protect that.

## Privacy

Nothing you enter is sent anywhere. The tool runs entirely in your browser — no cloud, no account, no data collection. What you type exists only on your screen, and it leaves when you do. If you choose to keep a copy, that file is saved to your own device and stays yours alone, to reopen whenever more of the story comes back to you. You can verify all of this yourself: the entire tool is one readable file.

## Running it

**▶ [Open The Landscape](https://ablackwellb.github.io/the-landscape/index.html)** — add a moment, place it, and watch your land take shape. Drag to orbit, scroll to rise or descend. Names emerge as you come closer.
Give it twenty unhurried minutes. Start anywhere — the map is never finished, because the life isn't.
